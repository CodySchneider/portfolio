Get Started

Our online application process provides an easy and secure way to open your new Checking Account in only 5-10 minutes.

To get started, you will need the following information for each account owner:

  • Social Security number
  • Driver's License number or State ID
  • Home address

Making a deposit is part of the process of opening your new account. If you want to transfer money from an account at another bank, you will also need the account information found on your checks or deposit slips.
